CALISTHENICS

it helps to build muscle mass, which can increase your metabolism and help you burn more calories throughout the day. This can lead to weight loss and improved overall health. Additionally, weightlifting can improve bone density, reduce the risk of injury, and improve balance and coordination.

WEIGHT-LIFTING

it is an effective way to build functional strength. Functional strength refers to the ability to perform everyday tasks such as lifting objects, pushing, pulling, and twisting. Calisthenics exercises such as push-ups, pull-ups, squats, and lunges all require the use of multiple muscle groups, making them excellent exercises for building functional strength.

CARDIO

it helps to improve your heart health. Regular cardio exercise can help to strengthen your heart and reduce your risk of developing heart disease. It can also help to lower your blood pressure and improve your cholesterol levels.
